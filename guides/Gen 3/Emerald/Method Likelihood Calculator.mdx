<Gist>
  Given a setup (map, advance, lead), calculate the Pokémon outcome for every
  method (Wild1-Wild5) and their likelihood.
</Gist>

**Use cases**:

- Understand why you never encounter your target Pokémon even if you hit the correct advance.
- Optimize your lead cycle speed to improve your odds of hitting your target Pokémon.
