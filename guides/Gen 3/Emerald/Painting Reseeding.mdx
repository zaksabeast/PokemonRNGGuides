## Prerequisites

- Access to the Battle Frontier.
- Know how to create a [Battle Video](/emerald-battle-video).
- Recommended: Having read [Overview of RNG Advancing Techniques](/emerald-advancing-rng-techniques).

## Overview

Painting Reseeding is a RNG technique to make the RNG state jump by millions of advances instantly, giving access to
RNG states that would normally not be reachable realistically.

By interacting with a painting in Lilycove contest hall, the RNG state is reseeded using the current visual frame counter. 
The RNG state before interacting with the painting has no impact on the new RNG state. 
Players don't need to have participated in a contest for the paintings to be accessible.

### Validating
Performing Painting Reseeding successfully requires a frame-perfect input.
The new value of the RNG state depends on the exact timing of the button press.
After performing it, players need to validate whether they successfully hit their target frames before painting.

The best way to validate is to catch a high-level Pokémon in Victory Road, with an egg as the party lead. 
The egg lead ensure that the caught Pokémon will be generated with [method Wild-1](/gba-methods-lead-impact).
Afterwards, the stats and nature of the caught Pokémon can be used to calculate what was the actual hit painting frame.
In some cases, multiple possible frames are could have been used to generate the caught Pokémon. 
Talking with the stat judge in the Battle Frontier can help determine the real hit frame. 
The stat judge says information about the sum of the IVs of a Pokémon, its highest stat and IV value.

### Post-Validating
After confirming that the wanted frames before painting was hit, players create a [Battle Video](/emerald-battle-video) 
to save and restore their RNG state in case they miss their target advances after painting for the Pokémon encounter.
