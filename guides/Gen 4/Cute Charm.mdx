## Intro

This guide explains how to use Cute Charm in the RNG process for shiny Pokémon. Familiarity with Gen 4 RNG, including initial seed and TID/SID RNG, is assumed.

## PID and Groups

Cute Charm increases your chances of encountering shiny Pokémon if you have RNG'd a specific TID/SID combo.

To use Cute Charm, choose the right PID based on your target's gender, gender ratio, and desired nature. Cute Charm doesn't affect Method 1 Pokémon, genderless species, or species with a 100% male or female gender ratio.

For shiny females, prioritize the nature. For shiny males, consider the specific gender ratio (e.g., Bulbasaur's 82.5/17.5) and match it with the right nature. Below are PID tables for each scenario.

## The RNG

For example, if you want a shiny male adamant Machop, which is 75% male, check the 3rd table. In PID Group 1, find the matching PID `0000004E`. RNG a TID/SID around this group.

1. Launch PokeFinder and Pandora's Box, input the required details, and confirm the PID is correct.
2. Perform TID/SID RNG on a new save file.
3. Advance in the game until you quickly obtain a Pokémon with Cute Charm.
4. Go to a location with your desired Pokémon (Machop in this example).
5. Use PokeFinder to search for a shiny Pokémon. Ensure you use the Cute Charm lead.
6. RNG and encounter the shiny Pokémon.

```
Cute Charm is useful for both shiny hunting and RNGing a TID/SID for a PID group. Remember that the gender ratio is important when using a female Cute Charm lead for shiny hunting.
```

## 乱数流程

如果你需要捕捉异色雄性固执性格腕力（性别比 75%），可以参考第三张表。在PID组合1中，查找与固执对应的 PID 0000004E，然后进行 TID/SID 乱数以匹配该组合。

1. 使用 PokeFinder 和 Pandora’s Box 计算适合的 TID/SID，以获得目标PID组合。
2. 新开存档进行ID乱数
3. 推进游戏进度直到能够捕获具有迷人之躯特性的宝可梦。
4. 前往目标宝可梦所在区域（比如腕力）
5. 使用 PokeFinder 计算想要的异色宝可梦，并将迷人之躯特性的宝可梦在队伍首位。
6. 乱数然后遭遇这只异色宝可梦

```
迷人之躯特性在捕捉野生异色宝可梦和通过乱数获取TID/SID以匹配特定PID的时候非常有用。需要注意的是，当使用携带迷人之躯的雌性宝可梦作为首发宝可梦捕捉野生异色宝可梦时，目标宝可梦的性别比例会直接影响异色概率。
```
