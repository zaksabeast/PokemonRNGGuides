<Step step={2}>

1. Set your console time to **<Id4ConsoleSetDateString />**
2. Simultaneously press `A` to set the console time and start the timer below.
3. Exit the settings on your console and restart your console.
4. When the first timer ends, press `A` to start the game.
5. Name your character as fast as possible, and get to the screen below.
6. When the second timer ends, press `A`.

<Id4Timer />

</Step>

<Step step={3}>

1. Open your trainer card and check your TID.
2. If it matches your target, congratulations! You RNG'd your TID!
3. If not, type your TID into the box below and click "Generate".
4. Click "Calibrate" on the TID with the smallest delay offset (should be the first result).
5. Try to hit your target again.
