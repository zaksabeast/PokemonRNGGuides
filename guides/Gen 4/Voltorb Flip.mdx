# Find your seed

1. Save in front of the Voltorb Flip board.
2. Soft reset your game and quickly load the save.
3. Use the PokeGear to find the current time.
4. Enter the time and date into the tool below.
5. Start a Voltorb Flip game. DO NOT flip any cards!
6. Press `B` to quit immediately to reveal all cards.
7. Match the board below with your game by clicking each card to set it.
8. Press "Generate" then select your seed from the list of possible seeds.

<FindVoltorbFlipSeed />

> [!TIP]
> If no results show up:
>
> - Double check your cards.
> - Increase your max delay.
> - Decrease your minute by 1, in case you viewed the PokeGear late.

</Step>

<Step step={1}>

# View boards

1. Choose the number of boards you want to generate.
2. Click "Generate" to view the boards.
3. For each board, flip all "1"s, then "2"s, and finally "3"s. Do not skip any cards or all future boards will be wrong!

> [!TIP]
> Use the memo feature to mark all cards in-game before flipping. This helps make sure you don't forget to flip a card.

</Step>
