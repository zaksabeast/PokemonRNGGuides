## Tools

- [PokeFinder](https://github.com/Admiral-Fish/PokeFinder/releases)
- [Mystic Timer](/mystic-timer)
- Pokémon Black/White cartridge
- A configured game profile (see our [calibration helper](/bw-calibration))

> [!WARNING]
> Gen 5 RNG only works for cartridges – Twilight Menu++/flashcards won't work.

## Step 1: Setup

Prepare your target seed and timing before starting the RNG.

1. Save in front of the gift.
2. Open PokeFinder → Gen 5 tab → "Static" → "Searcher".
3. Select the correct game profile.
4. Set the correct starter and all desired filters (shininess, IVs, ability, etc.).
5. Set Initial Advances to at least 5.
6. Set Max Advances as needed. Avoid setting it too high, as it can slow the search or make it impractical.
7. Leave Min/Max IV Advances at 0.
8. Generate results and choose a target.
9. Open your timer and set the `target second` based on the second in the Date/Time column.
10. Set your console date using the Date/Time column, but do not confirm yet.

> [!NOTE]
> On 3DS consoles, timing is less consistent and may take longer. See our [3DS Helper page](/3ds-helper) for more info.

## Step 2: RNG the Starter

Hit your seed and advance to the correct target.

1. Start the timer and confirm the time change at the same moment.
2. Launch the game exactly when the timer ends to hit the target Initial Seed.
3. Hold any required keypresses.
4. Advance by saving the game repeatedly until you reach the correct Advance.
5. Close the menu and press `A` to open the gift and choose the starter.

## Troubleshoot

- Check that your date and time are set correctly.
- If you miss your target despite following all steps correctly, Gen 5 may be hitting a different Timer0. Retry until you hit the correct one.

**Congrats! You've now got your Pokémon!**
