<Gist>
  These patches trick Pokemon Dream Radar into thinking it has Diamond, Pearl,
  Platinum, HeartGold, or SoulSilver inserted to unlock the Gen 4 Legendaries.
</Gist>

## Tools

- A 3DS with CFW (Custom Firmware) and cheats enabled
- [Instructions for installing CFW](https://3ds.hacks.guide/)

## International Dream Radar

1. Save the cheats below to `luma/titles/00040000000AE100/cheats.txt`.
2. Enable them using Luma's cheat menu.

```
[Spoof Diamond Cart Inserted]

001555FC 45414441
```
