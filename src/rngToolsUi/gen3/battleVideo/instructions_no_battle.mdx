1. Save in front of the leftmost Battle Tower clerk, with 3 eligigible Pokémon for the Battle Tower.
1. On the website, start the timers.
1. When the 1st timer reaches 0, press `START + SELECT + A + B` to soft reset the game.
1. Once in-game, quickly talk with the clerk to enter a Battle Tower challenge (either Level 50 or Open, it doesn't matter).
1. Follow her until you meet your opponent.
1. Wait while the opponent opening phrase is displayed.
1. When the 2nd timer reaches 0, press `A` to close the opponent opening phrase, and start the battle.
1. Either win or lose the battle (but don't forfeit). It doesn't matter how long the battle takes.
1. At the end of the battle, ask the clerk to record your last Battle Tower match on your Frontier Pass.
