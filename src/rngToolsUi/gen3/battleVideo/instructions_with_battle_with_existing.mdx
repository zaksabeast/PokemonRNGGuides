1. Stand on a patch of grass in Route 101, with 3 eligible Pokémon for the Battle Tower, a Pokémon knowing Sweet Scent and one knowing Fly.
1. Open your trainer card.
1. Select "Battle Record" in-game, and immediately after, start the timers on the website.
1. When the 1st timer reaches 0, press `B` to close the battle video. It must be closed before the first attack of the battle.
1. Open your Pokémon party. Move your marker on `SWEET SCENT` of a Pokémon, and wait.
1. When the 2nd timer reaches 0, press `A` to trigger a Sweet Scent battle.
1. Select to run from the battle, but don't fully exit the battle yet. Wait with the "Got away safely" message visible.
1. When the 3rd timer reaches 0, press `A` to exit the battle.
1. Fly to Battle Frontier and go to the Battle Tower by running.
1. Talk with the leftmost clerk to enter a Battle Tower challenge (either Level 50 or Open, it doesn't matter).
1. Follow her until you meet your opponent.
1. Wait while the opponent opening phrase is displayed.
1. When the 4th timer reaches 0, press `A` to close the opponent opening phrase, and start the battle.
1. Either win or lose the battle (but don't forfeit). It doesn't matter how long the battle takes.
1. At the end of the battle, ask the clerk to record your last Battle Tower match on your Frontier Pass.
