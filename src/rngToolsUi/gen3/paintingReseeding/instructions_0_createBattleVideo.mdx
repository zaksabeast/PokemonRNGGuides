
1. Save in front of a painting in the Lilycove contest hall, with 3 Pokémon eligible for Battle Tower, and one knowing Fly.
1. On the website, start the timers.
1. When the first timer reaches 0, press `START+SELECT+A+B` to soft reset the game.
1. Select your save file and wait while standing in the front of the painting.
1. When the 2nd timer reaches 0, press `A` to interact with the painting.
1. Close the painting and quickly leave the contest hall.
1. Fly to Battle Frontier and go to the Battle Tower by running.
1. Talk with the leftmost clerk to enter a Battle Tower challenge (either Level 50 or Open, it doesn't matter).
1. Follow her until you meet your opponent.
1. Wait while the opponent opening phrase is displayed.
1. When the 3rd timer reaches 0, press `A` to close the opponent opening phrase, and start the battle.
1. Either win or lose the battle (but don't forfeit). It doesn't matter how long the battle takes.
1. At the end of the battle, ask the clerk to record your last Battle Tower match on your Frontier Pass.
