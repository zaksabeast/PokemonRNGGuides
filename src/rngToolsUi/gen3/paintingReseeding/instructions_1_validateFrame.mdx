1. Save the game.
1. Place any Egg Pokémon at the first slot of your party. Withdraw a Pokémon knowing Sweet Scent. Ideally, if you don't own a Master Ball, place a Pokémon knowing False Swipe at the 2nd slot of the party.
1. Fly to Ever Grande City, and enter Victory Road 1F cave. Move 1 step away from the entrance, otherwise Sweet Scent will fail.
1. Open your trainer card.
1. Select "Battle Record" in-game, and immediately after, start the timers on the website.
1. When the 1st timer reaches 0, press `B` to close the battle video. It must be closed before the first attack of the battle.
1. Open your Pokémon party. Move your marker on `SWEET SCENT` of a Pokémon, and wait.
1. When the 2nd timer reaches 0, press `A` to trigger a Sweet Scent battle.
1. Catch the Pokémon.
1. Open the Pokémon summary and fill the fields on the website with the Pokémon stats. Press "Generate", then follow the instruction below the results.
