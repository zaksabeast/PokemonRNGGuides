1. If there's a result matching the target advance before painting with a Very High or High confidence rating, the painting attempt was successful!
   - Continue to the next section of the guide (Update the Battle Video).
1. If there are no results, or if none of them have a Very High or High confidence rating, make sure that the content of the fields are correct.
1. If there are multiple results with a Very High confidence rating (or multiple High ratings):
    1. On the website, set the Rare Candy count to 1, and give a Rare Candy to your Pokémon.
    1. On the website, remove Pokémon entries with stats that don't match the newly improved stats of the Pokémon.
    1. Repeat feeding the Pokémon additional Rare Candies and filter the entries until there's only one Very High confidence rating left (or only one High rating).
    1. Additionally, you can fly to the Battle Frontier, and talk with the stat judge in the building behind the Pokémon Center, for hints about the IVs of the Pokémon. On the website, remove Pokémon entries that don't match the hints.
1. Once there is only one result with a Very High confidence rating (or only one High rating), click on its "Calibrate" button.
   - This painting attempt was not successful, but the timers were adjusted to improve the odds for your next attempt. Go back to the first step "Create the Battle Video".
