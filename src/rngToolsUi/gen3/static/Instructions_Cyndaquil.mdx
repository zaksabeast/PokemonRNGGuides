### Additional Instructions for Cyndaquil

1. Stand in front of the Cyndaquil Pokéball.
1. Press `Start` to open the menu. This prevents the scientist NPC from advancing the RNG state.
1. Wait until there are 5 seconds left on the timer and close the menu with `B`.
1. Interact with the Pokéball until the confirmation screen.
1. When the timer reaches 0, press `A` to select "YES".
