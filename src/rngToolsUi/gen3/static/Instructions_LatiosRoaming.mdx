### Additional Instructions for Latios

Additional requirements:
 - Level 14-40 Pokémon.
 - Many Repels.
 - Master Ball or many Ultra Balls with a Pokémon to prevent Latios from fleeing (ex: Crobat with Mean Look or Wobbuffet).

1. Before beating the Elite Four for the first time, make sure you already own everything required to catch Latios.
1. After beating the Elite Four, you'll end up in your house. Go downstairs.
1. Go through the dialogues. When asked about a color, wait with the option "Blue" selected.
   
1. When the 2nd timer reaches 0, press `A`. Latios will be generated.
1. Fly to Mauville and go to Route 110.
1. Activate your Repel with a Pokémon with level 14 to 40 in the first slot of your party.
1. Run the grass about 20 times.
1. If Latios is encountered, catch it and follow the standard calibration process.
1. If not, then Latios was not in Route 110. Enter the Cycling Road then leave back to Route 110 to make Latias move to another map.
1. Repeat the last steps until you catch it.
