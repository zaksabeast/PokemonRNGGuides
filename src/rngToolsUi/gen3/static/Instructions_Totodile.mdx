### Additional Instructions for Totodile

1. Stand in front of the Totodile Pokéball.
1. Press `Start` to open the menu, and wait until there are 5 seconds left on the timer. This prevents the scientist NPC from advancing the RNG state.
1. Close the menu with `B` and interact with the Pokéball until the confirmation screen.
1. When the timer reaches 0, press `A` to select "YES".
