### Additional Instructions for Voltorb

1. Stand in front of the Voltorb Pokéball.
1. Press `Start` to open the menu, and wait until there are 2 seconds left on the timer. This prevents random RNG advances caused by surrounding objects.
1. Close the menu with `B`.
1. When the timer reaches 0, press `A` to select "YES".
