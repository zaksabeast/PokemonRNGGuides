1. In-game, stand next to the target Pokémon or NPC.
1. Open your trainer card.
1. Select "Battle Record" in-game, and immediately after, start the timers on the website.
1. When the 1st timer reaches 0, press `B` to close the battle video. It must be closed before the first attack of the battle.
1. Perform the necessary actions to prepare the encounter, so that the only thing remaining to trigger the encounter is pressing `A`.
1. When the 2nd timer reaches 0, press `A` to trigger the encounter.
1. Catch the Pokémon, even if it's not your target Pokémon. It will be used to calibrate.
1. Open the Pokémon summary and fill the fields on the website with the Pokémon stats. Press "Generate".
1. If there are no results or only results with Low confidence ratings, make sure the fields are correct.
1. Click on the "Update Calibrate" button of the result with the highest confidence rating (the first result). This will update the timers to improve your odds of catching your target Pokémon.
1. Repeat all the previous steps until you catch your target Pokémon.
