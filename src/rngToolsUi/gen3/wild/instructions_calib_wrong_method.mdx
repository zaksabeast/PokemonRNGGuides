
If you hit the target advance, but the obtained Pokémon is not the target Pokémon, this means you hit the wrong method. Try the RNG manipulation a few times. If this keeps happening, you have 2 options:

- Option 1: Find a new target Pokémon with better likelihood. 
   - Setups that require an Egg lead are the most consistent. It is possible only display setups with Egg lead by disabling "Recommended Setups?" and filtering "Leads".

- Option 2: Change your lead Pokémon to increase the likelihood of triggering the wanted method.
    - If your target method is **Wild-1**, you need a lead with a **faster PID**.
    - If your target method is **Wild-2**, you need a lead with a **slower PID**.
    - If your target method is **Wild-4** and the method you keep triggering is **Wild-1**, you need a lead with a **slower PID**.
    - If your target method is **Wild-4** and the method you keep triggering is **Wild-2**, you need a lead with a **faster PID**.
    - To catch a new lead Pokémon with a faster or slower PID, use the "PID cycle speed" filter. Use a filter of 18 for the fastest possible PID cycle speed, and 900 for the slowest possible.
